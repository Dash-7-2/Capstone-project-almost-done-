What is Cyber-Bullying? 


        Cyber-bullying is online harrassmant or verbal abuse. It is a form of 
        bullying that is the most dangerous, as you can almost never know who 
        somone is online. If you don't know you will be in constant fear, because
        technology is now so advanced, that it follows you. More than 32 percent 
        of teens and kids get bullied every year.

Anyone can get Cyber-Bullied 


 
    People Get cyber-bulied all the time. It can be your friend, a stranger, 
    or even adults.  Many adults get shamed for the amount of money they have, 
    or how much their body weight is. And you can also get cyber-
    bullied by anyone. On the internet, all you have to do is put a fake name
    and not one person can know who you are. Hence, you can get be a grown 
    adult and cyber-bullly an 8 year old.
